About Me

Having moved frequently during my childhood, including living abroad, I found my escape in the pages of books. This lifelong fascination with literature led me to study English Literature at the University of Sussex for both my undergraduate and master's degrees. It was in 2017 that I began writing poetry seriously, a pursuit that eventually connected me with the Verse First poetry group.

My debut novel, a futuristic thriller exploring themes of subterfuge, power, and payback, is due for release in 2026.
